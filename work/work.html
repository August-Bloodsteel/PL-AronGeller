Design-build

Our extensive experience with design-build projects allows us to create value and flexibility, which in turn
creates savings for our clients in both time and costs. As a general contractor using design-build delivery
systems, we have a unique background in preconstruction that most contractors don’t normally possess.

We have an extensive amount of experience in design reviews, constructability analysis, scheduling,
coordination, and value engineering in all construction types and building systems. With experience on more
than 20 design- build projects, we have learned to work closely
with owners and end users to create value, flexibility, and time savings in the preconstruction phase.

Communication is the key to any successful relationship and before we do any heavy lifting, we work hard to
ensure a smooth process at every stage of a project. Designs are reviewed and discussed to confirm that the
client is getting the best value.
Our Project Executives and Project Managers are adept at identifying issues early in the process before they
become costly changes down the road. Our Estimators will create and refine the program budget. Project
Managers work hand in hand with Estimators to make adjustments as needed.

Our team is creative and innovative in searching for solutions and alternatives for value engineering ideas. And
as creative builders, we are always searching for time and cost-efficient alternatives.
