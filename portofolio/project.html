White Cubes Ubud

Private hotel.

Start and end of construction: September 2015 – June 2017
The hotel is located in the middle of rice fields in one of the most picturesque locations of the Ubud village in the heart of the island.

These are 4 two-story buildings where there is a bedroom on the second floor with panoramic windows and a dining room on the first floor where there is everything you need for a short rest or for a long stay.
The hotel twice won the Discovery Channel Award in the category “Unusual Hotels of the World”
